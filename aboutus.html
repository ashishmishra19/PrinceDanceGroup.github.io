Prince Dance Group, a dance troupe based in Berhampur led by T. Krishna Mohan Reddy.

It has won a reality show India's Got Talent on an Indian TV channel "Colors". The group is unique that the members are from a remote part of India and most of them are from disadvantaged sections of different parts of Ganjam district.

The India’s Got Talent where it got all the popularity

Nikhil Chinapa and Ayushmann Khurrana were the hosts of the show and it was judged by Shekhar Kapur, Kirron Kher, and Sonali Bendre. 56 contestants took part in the show and after several hardships; it was the Prince Dance group who came on top. The Vishnu’s Dashavatara performance by them made them nation favorite along with the judges.

Prince Dance Group is rooted in a small town of Berhampur

It is in the eastern coastline of India, in the state of Odisha. Our group is composed of twenty-six daily wageworkers who enthralled the entire India with their fantastic performance – Krishna Act and went on becoming the winner of the reality –talent show, " INDIAS GOT TALENT"
